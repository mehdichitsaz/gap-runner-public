Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-28. This policy explains how Gap Runner handles user information, including ad integrations added in V2.

Contact Privacy Team Support Back to Home

Data collection

Gap Runner does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information directly through gameplay. Scores and progress are stored locally and optionally synced to Firebase under your anonymous session.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

V2 includes Google AdMob for banner ads and rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details. No ad is shown during active gameplay.
