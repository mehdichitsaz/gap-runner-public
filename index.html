Gap Runner

Gap Runner is a fast, skill-based mobile game with lives, ghost mode, collectible stars, unlockable themes and characters, and per-difficulty leaderboards. V2 is out now.

Download on App Store Get Support Read Privacy Policy

Lives & Ghost Mode

Collect hearts during runs and use them on collision — respawn in the nearest safe gap with 5 seconds of blinking invincibility. Hold up to 3 hearts per run.

Collectible Stars

Stars appear in four tiers — Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Rainbow — each with different point values. Cumulative stars across all runs unlock new themes.

Unlock System

Themes unlock via lifetime stars, characters via mode-specific best scores, and avatars via achievements. Three items can also be unlocked by watching a rewarded ad.

Leaderboards

Separate ranked leaderboards for Easy, Medium, and Hard — compete on the difficulty that suits your playstyle.
